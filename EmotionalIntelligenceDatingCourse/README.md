# Emotional Intelligence Dating Course: Navigating Uncertainty in Attraction

## Course Overview
This 4-week course is designed for individuals who struggle with uncertainty in dating and attraction - specifically when you cannot clearly determine or pursue romantic interests. Each week builds practical skills for developing emotional intelligence, self-awareness, and authentic connection strategies.

## Course Structure
- **Week 1**: Self-Discovery & Emotional Awareness
- **Week 2**: Communication & Boundary Setting  
- **Week 3**: Building Confidence in Ambiguous Situations
- **Week 4**: Authentic Connection & Moving Forward

## Learning Approach
Each week includes:
- Theoretical foundations
- Practical exercises
- Reflection prompts
- Actionable steps
- Weekly challenges

## Target Audience
This course is for anyone who:
- Feels uncertain about expressing interest
- Struggles to read social cues
- Experiences anxiety in dating situations
- Wants to develop healthier approach to attraction
- Seeks authentic connections over games or manipulation
