# Week 2: Communication & Boundary Setting

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this week, you will be able to:
- Express interest and intentions clearly without manipulation
- Set and maintain healthy boundaries in dating contexts
- Handle rejection and uncertainty with emotional resilience
- Practice active listening and authentic responding

## Daily Breakdown

### Day 1: Authentic Expression Foundations
- Exercise: "I Statement" practice for dating contexts
- Reflection: What fears come up when expressing interest directly?
- Practice: Rehearse expressing simple interest in low-stakes situations

### Day 2: Reading Social Cues Accurately
- Exercise: Non-verbal communication observation (in public settings)
- Reflection: Difference between reading cues vs. projecting desires
- Action: Practice observing without interpreting for 15 minutes

### Day 3: Boundary Identification & Setting
- Exercise: Personal boundary mapping (physical, emotional, time)
- Reflection: Where do you struggle to say no or yes?
- Script: Practice boundary phrases for common dating scenarios

### Day 4: Handling Uncertainty in Communication
- Exercise: Tolerating ambiguity without seeking reassurance
- Reflection: What does "waiting for clarity" feel like in your body?
- Practice: Sending clear messages then sitting with uncertainty

### Day 5: Rejection Resilience Building
- Exercise: Reframing rejection as information, not personal failure
- Reflection: What stories do you tell yourself when someone isn't interested?
- Action: Write a "rejection resume" - what each experience taught you

### Day 6: Active Listening Practice
- Exercise: Conversation practice focused entirely on understanding
- Reflection: How often do you plan your response while others speak?
- Challenge: Have one conversation where your goal is solely to understand

### Day 7: Weekly Review & Integration
- Review: What communication patterns shifted this week?
- Integration: Create your personal "authentic expression" guideline
- Preparation: Preview Week 3 themes on confidence building

## Weekly Challenge
Initiate one conversation where you express genuine interest or appreciation using only authentic statements (no pickup lines, games, or hidden agendas). Notice how it feels to communicate directly.
